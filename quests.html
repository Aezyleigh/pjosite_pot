The Quest Log

Buckle up, demigods, because the adventure starts here!

The Lightning Thief (Book 1)
“You shall go west, and face the god who has turned...”

Key Information:

Percy discovers he’s a demigod and quests to retrieve Zeus’s stolen lightning bolt.

The Sea of Monsters (Book 2)
“You shall sail into the Sea of Monsters...”

Key Information:

Quest to find the Golden Fleece to save Camp Half-Blood’s borders.

The Titan’s Curse (Book 3)
“Five shall go west to the goddess in chains...”

Key Information:

Percy joins a rescue mission to save Artemis and Annabeth, facing new enemies.

The Battle of the Labyrinth (Book 4)
“You shall delve in the darkness of the endless maze...”

Key Information:

Percy and Annabeth navigate Daedalus’s Labyrinth to stop Kronos’s invasion.

The Last Olympian (Book 5)
“A half-blood of the eldest gods shall reach sixteen against all odds...”

Key Information:

Percy leads the final battle against Kronos in Manhattan, fulfilling the Great Prophecy.
